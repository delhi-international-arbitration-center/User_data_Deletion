About Account Deletion

This page allows users of the DAW App by Coresphere100 Technologies to request deletion of their account and associated data in compliance with Google Play policies.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  • Send an email to our support team using the button below.
  • Include your registered email address and full name.
  • Your request will be processed within 7 business days.
Request Account Deletion

Data Deletion Policy

Upon confirmation of your request:

  • Deleted: Personal details, app usage data, and stored preferences.
  • Retained: Legal & transactional records, as required by law, for up to 180 days.
