Request Account Deletion

This page is for users of the DAW App by Coresphere100 Technologies who wish to delete their account and associated data.

Steps to Request Deletion:

  • Email us at support@coresphere100.com with the subject line: “Delete My Account”
  • Include your registered email address or user ID
  • We will confirm deletion within 7 business days

What Data Is Deleted:

  • Your name, email, and login credentials
  • Session history and feedback submissions
  • Any personal preferences or saved content

Note: Some anonymized analytics may be retained for up to 30 days for internal reporting.
